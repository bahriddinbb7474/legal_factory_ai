# User Roles

## admin

Manages users, roles, OpenRouter settings, model selection, budgets, and system configuration.

## director

Reviews high-risk legal conclusions, approves or rejects red-risk documents, and sees strategic legal and cost reports.

## chief_accountant

Reviews tax, payment, debt, and accounting-related legal conclusions. Can approve relevant red-risk matters.

## accountant

Works with debt, tax letters, invoices, payment questions, and accounting-related documents.

## hr

Works with labor questions, employment documents, orders, disciplinary matters, and HR templates.

## procurement

Reviews supplier contracts, import contracts, delivery terms, and negotiation documents.

## sales

Works with customer contracts, client debts, claims, and commercial correspondence.

## production

Asks questions about production-related obligations, certificates, safety, and operational compliance.
